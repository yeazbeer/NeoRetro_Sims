About NeoRetro Sims

NeoRetro Sims is a small virtual shop concept for retro pixel items: action figures, pixel keychains, 8-bit collectibles, posters, desk decor, and more. This project was created as a university web assignment to demonstrate HTML, CSS, and a bit of JavaScript with a classic 90s arcade aesthetic and a dark theme.

Design goals

  • Classic 90s retro feel with dark/pixel aesthetic
  • Simple navigation and an easy-to-read product grid
  • Minimal JavaScript: modal product view, filters, and search
